Diablo 4 Loot Filter Editor

A simple tool to open up a .yaml filter for aeon0's D4LF and edit it visually.

Your existing filters can be found next to your D4LF install in /config/profiles or separately in %userprofile%/.d4lf/profiles.

Updated for D4LF v5.1.2. Made by zbee 2024. D4LF made by aeon0 2023.
